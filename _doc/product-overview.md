# Product Overview

## Product Name
*(To be confirmed — working title: Educational Platform)*

## What It Is
A full-stack educational platform where instructors publish structured courses and students access learning content — video lessons, written notes, downloadable PDFs, and MCQ quizzes — through a single unified app. Content can be free or paid (freemium model), giving instructors a monetisable publishing channel and students a coherent, structured learning experience.

## Target Users

### Students (Primary)
Browse and enrol in courses, consume video and written content, take MCQ assessments, post queries in real-time chat, and receive push/in-app notifications about new lessons, quiz availability, and replies.

### Instructors / Admins
Publish and manage courses: upload videos, write notes, attach PDFs, build MCQ quizzes, and set content pricing (free or paid per course or module).

## Core Features
1. **Login & Signup** — Email-based authentication for both students and instructors.
2. **Video Lessons** — Streamed or downloadable video content organised by course and topic.
3. **Written Notes** — Rich-text lesson notes paired with each topic for in-depth reading.
4. **PDF Materials** — Downloadable and in-app viewable PDFs — worksheets, guides, and reference documents.
5. **MCQ Assessments** — Multiple-choice quizzes per topic or course; auto-scored with instant results shown to the student.
6. **Free & Paid Content** — Freemium content model: some courses and materials are freely accessible; premium content requires payment to unlock.
7. **Student Chat & Query System** — In-app messaging and query board where students post questions and receive answers from instructors or peers.
8. **Notifications** — Push and in-app alerts for new content releases, quiz availability, query replies, and general announcements.

## Positioning
An all-in-one edtech platform for tutors, training institutes, and independent instructors who want to deliver structured, interactive, and monetisable learning — without stitching together multiple separate tools.

## Brand Tone
Trustworthy, clear, and student-first. Warm but professional — like a knowledgeable teacher who makes complex things feel accessible and manageable.

## Monetisation Model
Freemium: some courses and materials are free to all registered users; premium courses or individual modules require a one-time payment or subscription to unlock. Instructors set pricing per course or per module.

## Scope (MVP)
Login / Signup → Browse courses (free & paid) → Watch video lessons → Read notes → View / download PDFs → Take MCQ quizzes → Post and answer queries via in-app chat → Receive push and in-app notifications.

## Verified Operations (North Star)
- **Lesson Completed** — student finishes a video lesson or reads a notes module in full
- **MCQ Submitted** — student completes and submits a quiz for scoring
- **Query Resolved** — a student question receives an accepted answer in the chat/query board
