The Problem & Solution

✕

The Problem

A teacher running a hybrid coaching center has two separate student worlds to manage — offline students who attend in person, and online students joining remotely — with no single tool built for either. Content ends up scattered across WhatsApp, YouTube, Google Drive, and random quiz sites. There's no professional home, no clean way to monetise, and no unified view of both cohorts.

✓

The Solution

A dedicated coaching-center app the teacher fully owns. Offline students get digital supplements to their in-person classes; online students get full remote access — videos, PDFs, notes, MCQ quizzes, and live chat queries — all from one professional platform with free and paid tiers built in.
