The Problem & Solution

✕

The Problem

Students juggle five separate tools — YouTube for videos, WhatsApp for questions, Google Drive for PDFs, random quiz sites for tests, and email for updates. There is no single structured learning environment, and instructors have no clean way to monetise the content they create.

✓

The Solution

One platform where instructors publish structured courses — video lessons, PDFs, rich notes, and MCQ quizzes — with free and paid access tiers. Students learn, get assessed, raise queries via real-time chat, and stay current through push notifications.
