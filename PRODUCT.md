# Harsh Commerce Academy — Product Brief

## Brand
- **Name:** Harsh Commerce Academy
- **Tagline:** Learn Smart, Score Better
- **Tone:** Trustworthy, warm, professional — like a knowledgeable teacher who makes complex things feel accessible. Student-first, never cold or corporate.

## What It Is
A hybrid educational coaching platform operated by a single teacher-owner. Serves both in-person and online students through one unified app. Students access structured learning content — videos, notes, PDFs, MCQ quizzes — under a freemium monetisation model.

## Users

### Teacher / Owner (Admin)
- Creates and manages all courses and content (videos, notes, PDFs, MCQs)
- Sets pricing: free or paid per course / module
- Manages both offline and online student cohorts
- Receives and responds to student queries via in-app chat
- Sends notifications / announcements

### Students (End Users)
- Browse and enrol in free and paid courses
- Watch video lessons, read notes, download/view PDFs
- Take MCQ assessments with instant auto-scored results
- Post queries and receive answers in real-time chat
- Receive push/in-app notifications for new content, quizzes, replies

## Core Features
1. **Login & Signup** — Email-based authentication for students and the instructor
2. **Video Lessons** — Streamed/downloadable videos organised by course and topic
3. **Written Notes** — Rich-text notes paired with each topic
4. **PDF Materials** — Downloadable and in-app viewable PDFs (worksheets, guides, references)
5. **MCQ Assessments** — Multiple-choice quizzes per topic; auto-scored with instant results
6. **Free & Paid Content** — Freemium model; premium content locked behind one-time payment or subscription
7. **Student Chat & Query System** — In-app messaging board; students post questions, instructors/peers answer
8. **Notifications** — In-app alerts for new content, quiz availability, query replies, announcements

## North Star Metrics
- Lesson Completed — student finishes a video or reads a notes module in full
- MCQ Submitted — student completes and submits a quiz
- Query Resolved — a student question receives an accepted answer

## MVP Scope
Login/Signup → Browse courses (free & paid) → Watch video lessons → Read notes → View/download PDFs → Take MCQ quizzes → Post and answer queries via chat → Receive in-app notifications

## Anti-References
- Avoid the cluttered, overwhelming feel of legacy LMS tools (Moodle, Blackboard)
- No dark-pattern upsells or aggressive pop-ups
- No unnecessary complexity — the teacher is the sole operator; keep admin flows simple
